During the pandemic, a new encoding scheme appeared: Base45.
It is designed specifically for QR codes, taking advantage of their Alphanumeric mode to produce smaller codes than Base64 would.

I wrote an encoder/decoder in pure Erlang. It implements what later became [RFC 9285](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/rfc9285/).

To include it in your project:

    {deps, [base45]}.

More details on the [project page](/projects/base45). Source code on [GitHub](https://github.com/ratopi/base45).
